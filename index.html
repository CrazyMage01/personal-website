Modular Day Builder

Problem: Daily optimization leads to decision fatigue and unmemorable "bit of everything" days.

Solution: Themed day templates with flexible time blocks and activity libraries. Users select a theme (Creative Sprint, Deep Focus, Explorer Mode, etc.) and customize with specific activities.

Daily Progress Celebration Bar

Problem: Over-optimization for productivity creates guilt around intrinsically valuable activities.

Solution: Unified progress tracking that celebrates physical, creative, social, learning, and rest activities equally. Gamified interactions with immediate dopamine feedback.
