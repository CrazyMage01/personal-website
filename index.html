Philosophy & Approach

You can't just lecture people about AI Safety, or any other important issue, and expect them to care. Real change happens when you create the right environment where people feel comfortable wrestling with difficult questions and developing their own insights. I've learned that facilitating curiosity and agency works better every time.

I love combining interdisciplinary thinking with real human connection â€” whether that's creating spaces for breakthrough conversations, helping people see problems from new angles, or simply being someone others feel comfortable opening up to.

What drives me most? Watching someone realise they can do way more than they thought to help solve humanity's biggest challenges and taking steps to ACTUALLY DO IT.
